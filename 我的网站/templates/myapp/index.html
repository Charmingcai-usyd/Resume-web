Research Experience

2020.1 - 2020.7

PMSM current predictive control based on disturbance torque observer
Second Author
Published in 2021 China Automation Congress (CAC)

Permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) is easy to be affected by torque ripple and dead time of inverter, in order to solve this problem, this paper replaces the traditional PI control with current predictive control. At the same time, a disturbance torque observer is introduced to estimate the voltage error caused by the disturbance torque of the motor model in real time. Finally, through MATLAB / Simulink and experiments, it is verified that the new system suppress external interference and improve the control performance of the motor.
